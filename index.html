Happy Birthday

...To someone who will always be special to me

Dear Joy,

Even though life took us on different paths, I just want to take a moment today to wish you a truly amazing birthday. You were an important part of my life, and I will always appreciate the memories we shared.

I also want you to remember something important. Please don’t forget all the advice I’ve shared with you over time. Always strive to be a better version of yourself and make it your great line of duty to make your parents proud, because they deserve to see you shine.

The space between us was necessary, not out of anger, but so we could both focus on our growth. I truly hope you stay focused too, especially on your education. It’s something no one can ever take away from you, and it will always be your strongest advantage.

I’ve always said this, and I still mean it. I never saw you as just my girl, but also as my sister. Someone I genuinely care about and want the best for.

Just because we don’t talk like before doesn’t mean we are fighting. It simply means life is moving, and we are growing in our own ways.

I want the very best for you in everything you do. Keep pushing forward, keep improving, and keep believing in yourself.

Happy Birthday once again, Joy Olaoyo Farah. ❤

— Llola
